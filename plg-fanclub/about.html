Who We Are

Our Story, Our People, Our Promise

PLG Fan Club is more than an association. We are a family bound by respect, welfare, celebration, and the belief that no member should stand alone.

Club History

From a Small Circle to a Strong Community

PLG Fan Club began as a small group of friends and supporters in Kentinkrono who wanted a better way to care for one another in times of need and in times of joy. What started around a few shared meetings has grown into a trusted community network that now supports families, celebrates major life milestones, and creates a sense of belonging for every member.

Over the years, we have built a culture that honours Ghanaian values of unity and responsibility. Whether we are attending a wedding, supporting a bereaved family, or hosting our annual gathering, we show up with heart, respect, and practical support.

Our mission remains clear: promote, support, and strengthen the welfare of every member.
