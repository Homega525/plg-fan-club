About Our Family

Built on Care, Culture, and Commitment

PLG Fan Club is a registered fan community based in Kentinkrono, Ghana. We were founded to create a supportive home where members are seen, supported, and celebrated in every season.

Our Shared Activities

What We Do Together

🕯️

Funeral Support

In difficult moments, we stand shoulder to shoulder. We coordinate welfare visits, practical help, and financial support to comfort families in mourning.

💍

Wedding Celebrations

We celebrate love with joy and unity, supporting members with planning help, attendance, and gifts that reflect our spirit of togetherness.

🎉

Annual Get-Together

Every year we gather proudly to reconnect, review our journey, and honour the people who keep our community strong.

🥳

Parties & Social Events

From birthdays to holiday hangouts, we create joyful spaces where members can relax, laugh, dance, and build lifelong friendships.
