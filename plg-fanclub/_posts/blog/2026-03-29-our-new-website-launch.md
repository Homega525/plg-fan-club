***Dear PLG Fan Club family,***

We are thrilled to announce that the Preeminent Ladies and Gents Fan Club has officially launched its website and is taking its first steps into the digital world!

This is more than just a website — it is your dedicated online home. A place where members can stay informed, connected and celebrated no matter where life takes you. We created this platform specifically for you, our family, so that no one ever has to miss out on important news, upcoming events or club announcements again.

**What you can find on our website:**

From today, you can visit us at plgfanclub.com to access everything you need to stay in the loop. Our website features a full events calendar where upcoming programs — from weddings and funerals to our beloved annual get-together — will be announced in advance. You will also find our club news and updates section where the executive team will regularly post important information, welfare updates and community highlights.

Members looking to join officially can also complete their membership registration directly on the site at any time.

**We're coming to [TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@plg_fan_club?is_from_webapp=1&sender_device=pc)!**

In addition to the website, PLG Fan Club will soon be launching its official TikTok account! We want to celebrate our community, share our moments and let the world see the warmth and togetherness that makes this club so special. Stay tuned for our handle announcement very soon.

**A word from the executive team:**

We have always believed that our strength lies in our unity. This website and our growing social media presence are extensions of that same spirit — ensuring every member feels seen, informed and proud to be part of the PLG Fan Club family. Together we rise.

Welcome to our online home. We are so glad you are here.
